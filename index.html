Who We Are

At Your Service

Family-run mobile catering van serving proper, no-fuss food at car boots and events across Essex and Suffolk. Quick service, generous portions, great prices.

Started in 2021, Booty Burgers has become a firm favourite across the region's car boot circuit. From our fully-equipped professional van we serve everything from classic burgers and hot dogs to full breakfast baps — all freshly made to order.

Available for private hire at weddings, birthday parties, corporate events, pub functions and more. We bring the van, the food, and the good vibes.
